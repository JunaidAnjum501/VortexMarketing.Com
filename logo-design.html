FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our logo design services.

Our standard logo design process typically takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish. This includes the discovery phase, concept development, revisions, and final delivery. For more complex projects or brand identity packages, the timeline may extend to 4-6 weeks. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your project requirements.

Our standard logo design package includes 3-5 initial concepts for you to review. After you select your preferred concept, we'll refine it based on your feedback with up to three rounds of revisions to ensure the final design perfectly matches your vision. If you need more concepts or revisions, we offer additional options at a reasonable cost.

We provide your final logo in all industry-standard formats for both print and digital use. This includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) for scalability, PDF for high-quality printing, and raster files (PNG, JPG) in various sizes for digital applications. We also provide versions with transparent backgrounds and in different color variations (full color, black, white, etc.) to ensure you have everything you need for all applications.

Yes, we offer comprehensive brand identity packages that include logo design plus additional brand elements such as color palettes, typography selection, brand guidelines, business card design, letterhead, social media assets, and more. These packages are customized based on your specific needs and can be tailored to fit businesses of all sizes, from startups to established companies looking for a rebrand.

Upon full payment, you will own all rights to your final logo design. We transfer complete ownership and intellectual property rights to you, allowing you to use, modify, and reproduce your logo however you wish. We may request permission to display your logo in our portfolio, but we will always seek your approval first and respect your decision.
